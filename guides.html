Boss Battles

Hexe Marie

A powerful and mysterious boss that requires careful strategy and timing to defeat. Hexe Marie uses dark magic and can summon minions to aid in battle.

  • Watch for dark magic attacks and dodge accordingly
  • Focus on eliminating summoned minions first
  • Attack during her spell-casting animations
  • Use fire-based weapons for increased damage

Queen Stoneback Crab

A massive crustacean boss with a heavily armored shell. This boss requires patience and precise attacks to break through its defenses.

  • Target the weak points on its underbelly
  • Dodge its powerful claw attacks
  • Use piercing weapons to break through armor
  • Attack from behind when it charges forward

Reed Devil

A swift and agile boss that uses speed and deception to overwhelm opponents. Quick reflexes and pattern recognition are essential.

  • Learn its attack patterns and telegraphs
  • Stay mobile and avoid standing still
  • Counter-attack after dodging its combos
  • Use area-of-effect attacks when it's grouped

Staglord

A massive beast with powerful charge attacks and area-of-effect abilities. This boss requires strategic positioning and timing.

  • Stay to the sides to avoid charge attacks
  • Watch for ground slam indicators
  • Attack the legs to stagger the boss
  • Use ranged attacks during its charge sequences

White Horn

A legendary creature with devastating horn attacks and ice-based abilities. This boss requires mastery of dodging and counter-attacking.

  • Dodge the horn charge attacks carefully
  • Break ice formations quickly to avoid damage
  • Attack the horn when it's stuck in the ground
  • Use fire-based attacks to counter ice abilities
