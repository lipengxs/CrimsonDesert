Kliff & Greymanes Character Guide

Use this page as a source-safe reading guide: distinguish what official material names or shows from story interpretation, fan theory, and claims that require a current primary source.

What This Page Can Confirm

Official character pages, trailers, store descriptions, and developer material can establish names, visible relationships, and the framing of a story. Keep each confirmed statement linked to a primary source when this page is expanded.

  • Use official spelling and presentation.
  • Quote only what a source clearly supports.
  • Separate a trailer scene from a full story conclusion.

What Requires Caution

Questions about leadership, allegiance, chronology, hidden motives, or late-game outcomes often turn a small character fact into a story claim. Do not assign a title or plot role merely because a search query expects an answer.

  • Label interpretation as interpretation.
  • Keep spoilers behind clear warnings.
  • Update after official story material changes.

How to Read a Character Page

Start with visible role and setting context, then move to gameplay or story notes only when the reader opts in. A useful character guide helps a new player understand why a person matters without copying a complete plot summary.

  • Use spoiler-light sections first.
  • Link to official media for context.
  • Archive superseded claims rather than hiding them.
