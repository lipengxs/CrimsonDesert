About Crimson Desert

Crimson Desert is a groundbreaking open-world action adventure game that combines intense combat with deep exploration in an immersive single-player experience. Set in the vast continent of Pywel, players take on the role of Kliff, leader of the Greymanes mercenary group, as he embarks on an epic journey to reunite with his scattered comrades and rebuild what was lost. The fate of Pywel now rests in the hands of skilled adventurers who can master the art of combat, exploration, and survival in this beautifully crafted world. Crimson Desert offers players the opportunity to experience authentic open-world gameplay, dynamic combat systems, and a rich narrative that unfolds across diverse landscapes and challenging encounters.

Story & Background

In Crimson Desert, players follow the story of Kliff and the Greymanes, a mercenary group that faces a devastating ambush by their sworn enemies, the Black Bears. After a brutal night attack leaves members of the Greymanes dead or scattered across Pywel, Kliff is determined to reunite with the survivors, rebuild the fallen Greymanes, and reclaim what was lost. The game features a rich narrative where different factions compete for control of valuable territories and mysterious powers. Each decision in Crimson Desert carries weight, as alliances are forged, dangers abound, and mysterious factions are uncovered. The world of Crimson Desert is filled with political intrigue, epic battles, and the constant struggle for survival in a land torn by war and ambition. As Kliff journeys across Pywel, he will come to realize that an unprecedented threat and a destiny greater than he imagined await him.
