# Crimson Desert SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 426 clicks, 18,741 impressions, 2.27% CTR, average position 8.58. The homepage ranks well, but a large share of demand is navigational “official website” intent and Japanese CTR is only 1.01%.

## 0–30 days

- Keep the prominent independent-fan-site disclosure and an official-site outbound link; do not target misleading official-identity snippets.
- Add `WebSite`, `VideoGame` and `BreadcrumbList` structured data with clear independent publisher identity.
- Reclassify obsolete pre-launch news as archive content and remove contradictory release messaging from current modules.

## 31–60 days

- Publish source-linked post-launch pages for editions, systems, bosses, maps, performance and patch impacts.
- Rework Japanese titles, descriptions and body copy as native Japanese editorial content.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Build backlinks through useful guides and creator assets, not paid or automated placements.
- Raise Japanese CTR above 2% and increase non-navigational organic clicks by 30%.
